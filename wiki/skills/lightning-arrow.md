# Lightning Arrow

A bow attack that fires a projectile arcing to up to 3 enemies within a large radius on
impact. Plays two roles in the [[Spiral Volley Deadeye]] build:

- **Levels 1-53**: main AOE clear
- **Level 54+**: dedicated trigger for [[Lightning Rod]] stacks (single target)

## Role transition at level 54

When swapping to [[Spiral Volley]] for clear, cut a fresh level 1 Lightning Arrow gem
purely for mana cost reduction. Its only job late is to chain into pre-stacked Lightning
Rods to trigger their pulses.

## Single target combo

1. Pre-stack [[Lightning Rod]] (up to 10) on the target
2. (Optional, level 41+) place Tornado Shot to absorb + re-emit arrows
3. Barrage immediately before
4. Spam Lightning Arrow — each chain into a Rod triggers a pulse

Each Rod pulses up to 8 times per chain. Tornado Shot lets arrows chain back from the
tornado to the rods, multiplying activations.

## Weapon swap

Placed on Weapon Set 1 alongside [[Lightning Rod]] in the build's weapon-swap setup.
