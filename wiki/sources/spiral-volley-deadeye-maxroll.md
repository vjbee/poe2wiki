# Spiral Volley Deadeye Build Guide (Maxroll)

## TL;DR

Maxroll build guide for a Ranger/[[Deadeye]] bow attack build. Uses [[Spiral Volley]] (29-arrow
360° AOE, level 54 unlock) as the primary clear skill, with [[Lightning Rod]] stacking + chained
[[Lightning Arrow]] for single target. The whole engine runs on the [[Resonance]] keystone, which
converts Endurance Charges to Frenzy Charges, letting Armour Break III + Enduring Impact II
self-sustain charge generation.

## Key claims

- [[Spiral Volley]] fires 29 arrows in a 360° arc, chains 6 times — single-click 1.5-screen clear
- Spiral Volley requires Frenzy Charges to deal full damage (fraction of damage without them)
- [[Resonance]] keystone converts Endurance Charges → Frenzy Charges; build does NOT work without it
- Armour Break III (linked to Spiral Volley) gives Endurance Charges on armour break — but only Physical Damage can break armour, so bow needs Physical Damage
- Enduring Impact II gives Endurance Charges on Heavy Stun (works on one-shot kills too)
- Snipe + Perfected Endurance generates first charge when out — keep Snipe at level 3 for low mana cost
- Switch [[Spiral Volley]] in at level 54 after allocating [[Resonance]]
- [[Deadeye]] ascendancy: Gathering Winds, Endless Munitions, Called Shots, Point Blank (early)
- Point Blank → swap to Far Shot at level 54 (Spiral Volley benefits more from Far Shot)
- Optional Pathfinder respec late-game for Running Assault knockdown immunity buff (patch 0.4.0 buff)
- Lightning Rod max stack: 10 — pre-stack on bosses for burst
- Tornado Shot (level 41) + Barrage multiply Lightning Rod activations
- Weapon swap: Set 1 = Lightning Arrow + Lightning Rod, Set 2 = Spiral Volley (order matters for Rigwald's Ferocity)
- Defensive plan: Evasion → Evasion/ES hybrid → ES + Ghost Dance in endgame
