# Pathfinder

Ascendancy for the Ranger class. Despite the Ranger's traditional bow identity,
Pathfinder can be played as a spellcaster — see [[Cold Spark Pathfinder]] for the
flagship example.

## Key nodes

- **Relentless Pursuit** — immune to ALL slows, including Chilled Ground and Temporal
  Chains map mods. Mobility pick.
- **Running Assault** — removes the 50% reduced movement speed penalty while casting,
  enabling true cast-while-moving. Also removes the heavy stun when hit while sprinting.
- **Path Seeker** — allows respecing the entire Ranger side of the passive tree to
  reach Sorceress nodes, while keeping access to Ranger-start movement speed nodes.
  This is what makes Ranger-caster builds viable.
- **Traveller's Wisdom** — converts +5 attribute nodes into 5% global defense, damage,
  or mana efficiency. Generally best value: Cost Efficiency (mana) or Global Defense.
  Pairs strongly with high-attribute items like Astramentis.

## Choosing first two nodes

- With leveling gear: take **Relentless Pursuit** + **Running Assault** for mobility
- Without leveling gear: take **Path Seeker** + **Traveller's Wisdom** for raw damage

## Why for casters

- Mana efficiency nodes help fund expensive end-game setups like Cast on Critical Comet
- Mobility nodes solve the "spell caster is stuck in cast animation" problem
- Path Seeker bypasses the geographically inconvenient starting position for spell scaling
