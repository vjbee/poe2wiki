# Entangle

Auto-targeting spell that spreads vines on the ground. Available from **level 1**. Druid-class skill, possibly part of the **Primal Skills** category introduced in [[patch-0.4.0]] (not explicitly tagged in the available source).

## Role

- **Clear**: spread vines across a pack so they overlap; detonate with [[Thunderstorm]] via the **Accelerated Growth** support.
- **Single target**: cast multiple Entangles into a boss, then detonate with [[Thunderstorm]] + spam [[Thrashing Vines]].

## Support linking (per [[Entangle Oracle]] build)

- **Accelerated Growth** — the detonation support; required for the Entangle + Thunderstorm combo to work.

## Scaling

The build guide's offensive priorities for Entangle are inherited from the Oracle build:

- +Physical / +Spell Gem Levels.
- %Spell Damage, %Spell Physical Damage.
- Cast Speed.
- Increased Duration (vines persist longer on the ground).
- Increased Area of Effect (more overlap on packs).
