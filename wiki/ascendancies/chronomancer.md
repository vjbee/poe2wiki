# Chronomancer

Sorceress ascendancy themed around time manipulation. The 0.5.0 "Return of the Ancients" launch reworks several existing nodes and adds at least one new notable (**Phased Form**).

**Status:** 0.5.0 preview. Information below is sourced from an official GGG announcement video.

## New notable — Phased Form

Take less damage from hits; a portion of that damage is **taken after a delay**.

Two notable properties:

- The delay applies **only to hits**. Damage-over-time effects "just straight up mitigated" — DoT does not feed into the delayed damage pool.
- Pairs with **Temporal Rift**: using Temporal Rift after taking a big hit recovers life to its prior value AND **erases the delayed damage** that was stored. The combo trivialises spike damage when the Rift is up.

## Reworked nodes

### Inevitable Agony

- New visuals and gameplay effects.
- Stored damage is now displayed on bosses' static life bars.
- When stored damage reaches a lethal level, the effect **triggers immediately** to wipe the enemy out instead of waiting on a later cycle.

### Rapid River — removed

The Rapid River passive (which sped up Recoup application) is gone. In exchange, **Recoup-speed investment has been distributed across the core passive tree** — so Recoup is no longer Chronomancer-locked; other classes can build into it as well.

### Time Snap + Time Freeze — both allocatable without full 8-point investment

Previously, taking both Time Snap and Time Freeze required spending all 8 ascendancy points. After 0.5, both can be allocated **independently**, freeing 0–6 points for other choices.

### Now and Again — reworked

Spell casts now have a chance to alter time and both **echo** and **repeat**. The two rolls are **independent**, so a cast can hit both — producing "a powerful burst of spells all at once".
