# Oracle

One of the two Druid ascendancies added in [[patch-0.4.0]] (the other is [[shaman]]). Caster-leaning ascendancy with plant-themed scaling and a tree-navigation toolkit centred on **The Unseen Path** and **Entwined Realities**.

## Known ascendancy nodes

From the [[Entangle Oracle]] build guide; ascendancy is broader than what's documented here.

- **The Unseen Path** — reveals special nodes on the passive tree.
- **Entwined Realities** — allocate high-value notables without pathing to them through the tree.
- **The Lesser Harm** — reduces hit damage; trade-off is lower crit chance.
- **Forced Outcome** — at "25% – 35%" investment, reroll crits to scale damage.

## Playstyle fit

Suited to spell casters that want a mix of damage scaling and defensive layers. The Unseen Path + Entwined Realities combination acts as a tree-skip — pick a notable and you do not have to spend points connecting to it.

The Lesser Harm offers a flat hit-reduction defensive layer with a built-in cost (lower crit), which suits high-cost / low-frequency cast patterns where crit chance is not the primary scaling axis.

Forced Outcome leans into the trade-off: spend on the right thresholds and crits actively scale damage rather than being a flat multiplier.

## Builds using Oracle

- [[Entangle Oracle]] — vine + Thunderstorm caster; uses Unseen Path → Entwined Realities → The Lesser Harm → Forced Outcome.
