# Trial of Chaos

Endgame Trial system in Path of Exile 2 featuring rotating boss encounters. Patch 0.4.0d retuned several of the bosses inside.

## Bosses (referenced in 0.4.0d patch notes)

- **Bahlak, The Sky Seer** — overall damage lowered.
- **Uxmal, the Beastlord** — overall damage increased.
- **Vornas, the Fell Flame** — damage reduced.
- **Morvak, the Infernal** — damage reduced.
- **Quadrilla** (all monster variants) — damage increased.

## Notes

The 0.4.0d adjustments are a tuning pass, not a structural change to the Trial. Bahlak and the two flame-themed bosses were softened; Uxmal and the Quadrilla pack were sharpened — suggesting Bahlak and Vornas/Morvak were over-tuned relative to clear targets, while Uxmal and Quadrilla content was being skipped or trivialised.
