# Gemling Legionnaire

Mercenary ascendancy. The 0.5.0 "Return of the Ancients" launch brings two new ascendancy skills — **Advanced Thaumaturgy** and **Virtuous Barrier** — that lean further into the Gemling's identity as a gem-quality specialist.

**Status:** 0.5.0 preview. Information below is sourced from an official GGG announcement video; numbers may shift between preview and live release.

## New ascendancy skill — Advanced Thaumaturgy

Every socketable skill gem in the game gains an **additional quality option** once Advanced Thaumaturgy is allocated. Hold **Alt** while hovering a gem to preview the new bonus.

The bonuses are described as "amazing effects" — well beyond stat-only quality. Examples called out in the announcement:

- **Fireball** — instead of firing 2 additional projectiles, fires **up to 10 in a circle**. Combined with Fire Infusion, the ring of fireballs explodes into another ring of smaller fireballs.
- **Explosive Shot** — gains **2 more rounds in its magazine** (no reload needed between shots until empty).

GGG references "over 200 different skill gems" gaining additional quality bonuses through this node — i.e. effectively every gem in the game.

## New ascendancy skill — Virtuous Barrier

A defensive skill that conjures a barrier from the Gemling's socketed skill gems. Each socketed gem **empowers the barrier**, with the strength of the effect depending on the gems' **required attributes**.

This is the first explicit defensive layer added to Gemling Legionnaire in this preview cycle and addresses the longstanding "much needed defences" gap for the ascendancy.
