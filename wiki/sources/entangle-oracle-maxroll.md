# Entangle Oracle Build Guide (Maxroll)

## TL;DR

Maxroll's flagship endgame Druid build for the 0.4 cycle. Oracle ascendancy. Plant-themed: spread [[Entangle]] vines, "water" them with [[Thunderstorm]] to detonate via the **Accelerated Growth** support; [[Thrashing Vines]] for single-target. Late-game pivots from a standard Life-based caster to a high-cost spell engine driven by **Mind Over Matter** + **Eldritch Battery** — Energy Shield converts to Mana that absorbs damage before Life, with [[Atalui's Bloodletting]] making spells cost Life instead of Mana.

## Key claims

- **Core combo**: [[Entangle]] (auto-target, level 1) + [[Thunderstorm]] (detonation, level 14). The Accelerated Growth support causes Thunderstorm hits on vines to produce large explosions.
- **Single-target**: [[Thrashing Vines]] (level 31), linked with Heft + Considered Casting. Default vine limit is 1; raised via Idol of Uldurn or Overabundance I.
- **Oracle path**: Unseen Path → Entwined Realities → The Lesser Harm → Forced Outcome.
- **Key keystones**: Wildsurge Incantation (campaign), Mind Over Matter + Eldritch Battery (endgame).
- **Endgame conversion**: Life-cost casting via Atalui's Bloodletting + Lifetap unlocks the MoM/EB combo, making Mana act as a second life bar fed by Energy Shield.
- **Key uniques**: Bushwhack (campaign, Pin), Idol of Uldurn (single-target Limit), Atalui's Bloodletting, Atziri's Rule (staff with spell life leech corruption), Kurgal's Gaze (helmet doubling life-regen nodes), Undying Hate (jewel, Tecrod variant).

No contradictions with the existing two Ranger builds — disjoint class and ascendancy. First non-Ranger build in the wiki.
