## Spiral Volley Deadeye Build Guide for Path of Exile 2 The Last of the Druids 0.4.0

The Spiral Volley Deadeye Build Guide centers around a skill that rapidly shoots a flurry of 29 arrows in a 360 degree arc around you, which then chain 6 times between enemies. This means just one button press has the potential to clear 1.5 screens in every direction, making it ideal for fast mapping.

With a particular focus on extremely efficient map clear, Spiral Volley excels at comfortably completing as many maps per hour as possible. Resonance paired with Armour Break III and Enduring Impact II completely trivialize charge generation the moment you reach the required damage threshold, allowing you to freely spam Volley.

For single target the build shoots multiple Lightning Rods into the ground before rapidly triggering their pulses with Lightning Arrow.

This build can also be played as a Pathfinder to utilize Patch 0.4.0's buffs to Running Assault, which prevents you from being knocked down while Sprinting entirely. It's recommended to start as a Deadeye and then respec to Pathfinder once your gear is good enough. This then allows you to utilize the risk-free +120% Movement Speed sprint without running into damage problems.

## Skills

Early Access

Spiral Volley Deadeye

- Spiral Volley quickly fires 29 arrows in a 360 degree arc, achieving the most efficient screen clear with a single click in the game. Having Frenzy Charges when you use the skill is very important as it does a fraction of the damage without them.
- You generate Frenzy Charges by first generating Endurance Charges with Armour Break III and Enduring Impact II linked to Spiral Volley. These then get converted to Frenzy Charges thanks to the Resonance Keystone.
	- The two separate Frenzy sources mean that it's likely that each Spiral Volley cast refunds some, is not all Frenzies as long as you hit a few monsters with it. Serpent's Egg further increases the amount of charges gained.
		- Snipe linked to Perfected Endurance generates the first charge whenever you run out. Keep this at level 3 for lower mana cost.
- Lightning Arrow fires a projectile that arcs to up to three enemies within a large radius on impact. Until level 54 this is your main source of AoE clear. Afterwards, it's adjusted to exclusively trigger your Lightning Rods. Cut a new level 1 Lightning Arrow for mana purposes when you swap to Spiral Volley.
- Lightning Rod shoots a Rod at the target location which deals damage each time your Lightning Arrow chains to it. Until 54 this acts as both your clear and single target. Afterwards it is used purely for single target damage, as pre-stacking multiple Rods before shooting them with Lightning Arrow deals enormous damage.
- Check out the Skill Rotation section for information on proper skill usage!

- Herald of Thunder is a passive buff that triggers effects upon killing shocked enemies. It provides a substantial increase in clear speed.
- Wind Dancer is a passive aura that massively increases your evasion.
- Use Tame Beast to enable the passive nodes on your tree that grant you damage when near a companion. Note that the Spirit cost is not static as beasts of different potency reserve different amounts. Your best option is a beast with the Haste Aura for a substantial boost to your clear speed and damage. You can keep it socketed, but inactive until you do.
- Charge Regulation grants you more Critical Hit Chance, skill speed and occasionally more Defenses when Charge Profusion II grants an Endurance Charge.
- Ghost Dance grants Ghost Shrouds. When you are hit, a stack is consumed recovering Energy Shield based on your Evasion rating. This is only used in the Endgame setup after switching to ES.

- In the early Acts Freezing Salvo provides a powerful burst of damage to take care of any tanky enemies as well as large hordes.
- Use Barrage to empower your Lightning Arrow to rapidly shoot multiple arrows and increase your Lightning Rod burst.
- Tornado Shot creates a stationary tornado, which absorbs any projectiles shot into it before spitting them back out. This is extremely useful as it allows your Lightning Arrow to Chain from the Tornado back to your Lightning Rods, massively boosting your Lightning Rod activations.
- Check out the Skill Rotation section for more information on proper skill usage!

When talking about Attributes in this section, this includes all of them: Dexterity/Strength/Intelligence. This is because even if you might need Intelligence, by getting Dexterity, you can now undo the Dexterity nodes on your Passive Tree and turn them into Intelligence, having the same effect! ## Gearing

Early Access

Spiral Volley Deadeye

- During the campaign, try to get Life, Evasion and Resistances on as many slots as possible. This is going to be your early source of defences.
- Flat damage on Bow, Quiver, Jewelry and Gloves can provide huge boosts to both your clear and single target. Keep an eye out for these stats on items as you progress.
- Make sure you're continuously upgrading your gear as higher level bases provide substantial increases in both defence and damage values.
- Bow bases that grant a chance to fire an additional Arrow are useful but not a strict requirement.
- Socket your bows with Storm Rune or other elemental/physical Runes that are available for more damage.
- Use other elemental runes on your good Armour pieces for extra resistances.[Equipment](https://maxroll.gg/poe2/planner/0mvh0qyx)

Spiral Volley Deadeye Campaign Gear

- As you enter the Atlas of Worlds and begin mapping, your first goal is to cap your resistances and meet Attribute requirements.
- Prioritize upgrading your bow as it is your most important source of damage.
	- **Aim for high Physical Damage on your bow as only Physical Damage can break Armour to enable Frenzy Charge generation through Armour Break III!**
- After that, focus on increasing your life, energy shield and evasion to ensure you have the defenses to complete red maps efficiently.
- Look to pick up a Jewels that grant 2% mana/life on kill to substantially boost defenses and mana sustain.
- Pick up a Serpent's Egg as soon as possible to triple your charge generation for a much smoother and powerful experience. Save up for this as soon as you can.
- Cadiro's Gambit is an interesting option as Spiral Volley shoots 29 Arrows, guaranteeing every effect to be present. It grants the following:
	- **Crescendo**: Arrows chain 6 additional times and deal 60% increased Damage for each time they have Chained.
		- **Splinter**: Split towards 6 targets.
		- **Reversing**: Pierce all targets and Return to you.
		- **Diamond**: All Hits are Critical Hits with 60% increased Critical Damage Bonus.
		- **Covetous**: Enemies killed drop items with 600% increased Rarity.
		- **Blunt**: Cause 600% increased Stun Buildup.[Equipment](https://maxroll.gg/poe2/planner/0mvh0qyx)

Spiral Volley Deadeye Early Maps Gear
