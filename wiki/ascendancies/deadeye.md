# Deadeye

Ascendancy for the Ranger class. Focuses on projectile/bow scaling and mobility.
Compare to [[Pathfinder]] which trades projectile damage for mobility + mana efficiency
and enables spell-caster Ranger variants.

## Key nodes (Spiral Volley setup)

- **Gathering Winds** — stacking buff: increased Movement Speed, Attack Speed, and a
  large bonus to Evasion. Always-on QoL.
- **Endless Munitions** — extra projectile. For Spiral Volley Deadeye, this directly
  reduces [[Lightning Rod]] ramp time (more arrows per attack = more pulses).
- **Called Shots** — Deadeye-exclusive persistent buff that periodically auto-casts
  the socketed Mark. In the [[Spiral Volley Deadeye]] build, slot Sniper's Mark — with
  [[Resonance]], this becomes a reliable Power Charge source for Charge Regulation, and
  Charge Profusion II occasionally grants Endurance Charges → Resonance → Frenzy.
- **Point Blank** — projectiles deal more damage to nearby enemies. Strong early.

## Critical Point Blank → Far Shot swap at level 54

When swapping to [[Spiral Volley]] for clear:

- **Respec Point Blank → Far Shot**
- Spiral Volley benefits much more from Far Shot because its arrows volley outward in
  a 360° arc

Forgetting this swap kills a meaningful chunk of clear damage.

## Why Deadeye for Spiral Volley

- Endless Munitions stacks directly with Spiral Volley's arrow count
- Called Shots automates Mark uptime → feeds the [[Resonance]] charge loop
- Gathering Winds covers mapping QoL (movement speed)
