### \--SKILL ROTATION--

#### \------------------------Stormblast Bolts VARIANT----------------------

#### Single Target

1. 4-5x Stormblast Bolts as prep  
	most bosses yap before they can be damaged lol
2. **wait a few seconds** then **dodge roll** to trigger Mirage Archer (detonates Stormblast Bolts)  
	waiting ~3s bypasses boss "one shot dmg reduction"
3. spam Stormblast Bolts until reload  
	stand **CLOSE TO ENEMY** (Close Combat II)
4. Emergency Reload
5. continue spamming Stormblast Bolts

#### Clearing

1. spam Galvanic Shards  
	Herald of Thunder does the rest

#### \----------------------Shockburst Rounds VARIANT------------------------

#### Single Target

1. charge Plasma Blast **FULLY**  
	hold while boss is yapping; fire when done
2. spam Shockburst Rounds until empty mag  
	enemy **MUST BE** **SHOCKED**
3. Emergency Reload
4. continue spamming Shockburst Rounds  
	if shock expires, reapply w Plasma Blast

#### Clearing

1. spam Galvanic Shards  
	Herald of Thunder does the rest

### \--WEAPON SET CONFIGURATION--

to configure weapon sets, you **must do** **ALL** **of the following**:

1. in your **Inventory**, **right click** the " **I** " **Roman Numeral** above your weapon to **LOCK IT TO BOTH SETS**  
	this saves you from needing to **acquire an entirely new weapon**  
	once done correctly, you will see a **silhouette of your weapon next to itself**
2. for certain **skill gems**, you must **allocate them to a specific set** - do this by clicking the " **\>** " for a given skill and, in the new menu that appears, **unchecking** either "I" or "II", depending on **which set it should** **ONLY** **be on**.

importantly, after doing all of this, you will have a **SEPARATE KEYBIND** **FOR** Stormblast Bolts  
you will see the " **II** " in the **top-right** of the **correct** Crossbow Shot **'s icon**  
I like to put this on middle-mouse but you can put it wherever's comfortable

the details for **which gems go in which set** are as follows:

#### Weapon Set I

ensure the following skills are **ONLY on Set I** (uncheck "II"):

- Galvanic Shards
- Herald of Thunder

#### Weapon Set II

ensure the following skills are **ONLY on Set II** (uncheck "I"):

- Stormblast Bolts
- Mortar Cannon
- Attrition

in a **FRESH START** scenario**, ignore the unique items** (obviously)

Rampart Raptor OP **early on** - get one with **3 sockets** (4 if you're $$$)  
use Lesser Glacial Runes (swap to regular Glacial Rune at level 15)  
use Blackheart rings for base dmg until you get decent **rares** with " **\+ cold / phys / lightning dmg** "

around **Act 3** Rampart Raptor will fall off, so **buy a decent rare crossbow** at that point  
you'll also be swapping to Stormblast Bolts, so start prioritizing **lightning dmg** mods

you're simply looking for as high **phys** / **lightning** dmg + **attack speed** as possible on a **rare crossbow** and **life** / **res** everywhere else  
try to also get some **flat dmg** (rings & gloves) and, of course, " **+lvl skills** " (weapon, amulet)

get as high a roll of "%all ele res" as possible on Skin of the Loyal  
this offsets Rondel of Fragility

replace Meginord's Girdle, Northpaw & both Blackhearts with rare gear with **life** & **%res** whenever you feel like it  
get some **flat dmg** (+phys / +lightning) on rings, too!

anoint Unbound Forces or Acceleration (more $$)

### \--FRESH START SCENARIO--

you won't have access to the exact supports early on, therefore, use the following links:  
Permafrost Bolts  
Rapid Attacks I > Elemental Armament I > Fork (only if you get an **early Lesser Jeweler's Orb**)  
Fragmentation Rounds  
Elemental Armament I > Cold Attunement

**<><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><><>**

use Permafrost Bolts x Fragmentation Rounds for **Acts 1 & 2**  
freeze them with Permafrost Bolts, then explode the freeze with Fragmentation Rounds  
you can also use good 'ol Crossbow Shot for **single target,** if you want

for your first Spirit gem (Act 1 **Freythorn** ), take Herald of Ice (Magnified Area II > Elemental Armament II > Elemental Focus)  
you don't need Herald of Thunder until you start using Galvanic Shards

as soon as you get a **t9 gem**, take Stormblast Bolts and **proc it** **manually** with Explosive Shot  
after a **t11 gem** drops, proc with Mortar Cannon x Explosive Grenade instead

this is also when you switch over to Galvanic Shards, dropping Permafrost Bolts and Fragmentation Rounds entirely

finally, once you acquire your final +Spirit (Interlude Act 3), you can drop Mortar Cannon and enable Mirage Archer in Set II to automatically proc Stormblast Bolts

you **MUST** have your **Weapon Sets configured properly** for Mirage Archer to work!  
details in the " **Build Variants** " section
