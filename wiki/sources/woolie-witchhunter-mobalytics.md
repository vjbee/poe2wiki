# Woolie's Run n Gun Witchhunter (Mobalytics)

## TL;DR

Mercenary / Witchhunter crossbow build. "Shoot crossbow, things die." Two single-target variants: **Stormblast Bolts** (primary) and **Shockburst Rounds** (deprecated). Clear is always **Galvanic Shards** + **Herald of Thunder**. The endgame trick is using **Mirage Archer** with **Explosive Shot** on a separate weapon set to auto-proc Stormblast Bolts — a dodge roll triggers the detonation. Defensive identity: **Sorcery Ward × Ceremonial Ablution** converts 30% of Evasion to HP for a high evasion + life hybrid that bypasses Rondel of Fragility's downside via a high all-ele-res roll on Skin of the Loyal.

The guide is **0.4 TLotD verified** with forward-looking notes for 0.5 (author expects no significant changes, possibly minor Stormblast Bolts nerfs).

## Key claims

- **Single-target loop (Stormblast Bolts)**: pre-stack 4–5 bolts during boss "yap" phase, **wait ~3 seconds** then dodge roll — the wait bypasses boss "one shot damage reduction" (see [[boss-anti-burst]]), and the dodge proccs Mirage Archer + Explosive Shot which detonates the stack. Continue spamming with Emergency Reload as needed.
- **Clear**: spam [[Galvanic Shards]]; Herald of Thunder handles the rest.
- **Stand CLOSE**: Close Combat II support requires proximity for damage.
- **Weapon set configuration is mandatory** for the endgame Mirage Archer auto-proc — Set I (Galvanic Shards, Herald of Thunder), Set II (Stormblast Bolts, Mortar Cannon, Attrition). Lock the weapon to both sets via the Roman-numeral toggle in inventory.
- **Ascendancy order**: Zealous Inquisition → Obsessive Rituals → Ceremonial Ablution. **Unspec Zealous Inquisition** after the final trial — leveling-only.
- **Anointment**: Unbound Forces (budget) or Acceleration (expensive).
- **Defensive notable stack**: Sorcery Ward + Ceremonial Ablution — "30% of Evasion = HP". Hyrri's Ire is the chase chest unique for further evasion scaling.
- **Skin of the Loyal**: stack high "%all ele res" roll to offset Rondel of Fragility's drawback.
- **Cross-references with existing wiki**:
  - [[Witchhunter]] is the **Mercenary ascendancy** paired with [[gemling-legionnaire]] (covered in [[ascendancy-changes-0.5-preview]]).
  - The "wait 3s to bypass one-shot reduction" tactic confirms [[boss-anti-burst]] applies to the bosses this build kills.

## Leveling shape

- Acts 1–2: Permafrost Bolts + Fragmentation Rounds (freeze + shatter).
- Tier 9 gem (≈Act 2): switch to [[Stormblast Bolts]], proc manually with Explosive Shot.
- Tier 11 gem (≈Act 3): proc with Mortar Cannon + Explosive Grenade.
- Act 3 ≈ swap Rampart Raptor for a rare crossbow.
- Interlude Act 3 (130 Spirit, +30 from gear): enable [[Mirage Archer]] auto-proc; drop Mortar Cannon.
