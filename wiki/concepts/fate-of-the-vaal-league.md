# Fate of the Vaal League

The current league overlay introduced in patch 0.4.0 (Dec 12, 2025), running through the 0.4 cycle. Vaal-themed: ancient Vaal civilisation remnants scattered across Wraeclast act as a campaign-and-endgame-spanning content layer leading to a customisable temple of Atziri.

## Core loop

1. Find Vaal structures in zones (campaign + endgame).
2. Sacrifice afflicted monsters at structures to power Vaal devices.
3. Complete **beacons** to gather energy.
4. Travel to **Lira Vaal**, the ancient temple of Atziri.
5. Place rooms in the Temple to construct a layout; adjacency between compatible rooms levels them up, adding new challenges and rewards.
6. Use accumulated energy to fuel further Temple expansion.
7. Final goal: confront **Atziri**, Queen of the Vaal, who is risking her civilisation for eternal youth via The Beast.

## League variants

- Standard, Hardcore, and Solo Self-Found versions exist; private leagues are also available. All share the same core mechanics and items.

## Adjacent mechanics

- **Holten league mechanic** — sub-mechanic involving Energized Crystals (re-enabled in 0.4.0d after a placement variance fix).
- **Atziri's Chambers** — central Temple room. After 0.4.0d connects to any adjacent room placed next to it.
- **Temple Destabilisation** — keyword that controls dispersion between random destabilisation and room-completion-triggered destabilisation (clarified in 0.4.0d).
- **Transcendent limbs** — drops from Temple content (loss-on-death bug fixed in 0.4.0d).
- **Temple Medallions** — drops in Temple instances (visibility and couch-co-op handling fixed in 0.4.0d).

## 0.4.0d changes

- Players who die during the Architect or Atziri boss fights can now respawn.
- Holten placement variance improved.
- Atziri's Chambers connects to any neighbour room.
- Temple now requires at least 1 placed room per run before opening.
- Temple Mods list aligned with the in-map Mods list.
- Temple Map UI now indicates completed and unreachable rooms.
- Description text added to Atziri's Chambers explaining unlock requirements.
- Several couch-co-op and visibility bugs fixed.
