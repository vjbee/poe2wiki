# Slow cap

Monsters cannot be slowed below **25% of their base movement / action speed**. Formalised as a keyword in patch 0.4.0d.

This is a floor on stacking slow effects — chill, temporal chains, hindering effects, and any other "X% reduced action speed" sources combined can take a monster down to but not below 25% of base.

## Implications

- Slow-stacking builds (chill + temporal + hex layering) hit diminishing returns earlier than the raw modifier math would suggest.
- Pinnacle bosses already have slow resistance on top of this floor.
- Player-side slow immunity (e.g. [[Pathfinder]]'s **Relentless Pursuit**) is unaffected — the cap applies to monsters being slowed by the player, not vice versa.

## Open questions

- Whether this floor stacks with boss-specific slow resistance or supersedes it.
- Whether 0.5.0 retains the 25% number.
