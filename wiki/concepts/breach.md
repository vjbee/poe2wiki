# Breach

Atlas content layer in Path of Exile 2. Brief 0.4 baseline: breach is one of the standard endgame layers (alongside [[abyss]], delirium, ritual, expedition). In **0.5.0 "Return of the Ancients"** it is rewritten into a multi-stage quest line plus a permanent endgame crafting ecosystem.

**Status:** This page reflects the **0.5.0 preview** (announced May 21, 2026; launches May 29). Pre-0.5 breach was a simple layer; the structure below is the rework.

## Mechanic changes (0.5)

- Open breaches now show a **visible duration timer**.
- Each monster kill visibly extends that duration.
- If the breach is kept open long enough, it **stabilises** and enters a new wave: tough Hiveborn miscreations spawn, and the breach cannot close until they are killed. They drop **hive blood**.
- Sometimes a **Marshall of Zest** (deadly Hiveborn elite) spawns; it drops **womb gifts**.

## Quest line (0.5, one-time)

1. First endgame breach — meet **Alith**, a member of the **Keepers of the Flame**.
2. Travel to the keepers' **monastery** south of the starting Atlas region.
3. Inside the monastery: the **Genesis tree** (see [[genesis-tree]]) — a Hiveborn artifact gifted by **the Dreamer**, currently withered.
4. Farm **hive blood** from stabilised breaches and **womb gifts** from Marshall of Zest spawns to restore the tree.
5. Use the Genesis tree to craft a **breach stone** from a womb gift.
6. Apply the breach stone to the Hiveborn foothold (see [[breach-domain]]).

## Endgame loop (0.5, repeatable)

- After the quest, **breach domains** appear randomly on the Atlas. Each requires a fresh breach stone, then play through breach hives + skyhives + breach lord kill.
- The Breach Atlas tree gains a new set of nodes:
  - Spawn breach hives outside of Hiveborn domains (on regular Atlas).
  - Grant Alith additional abilities for hive encounters — e.g. force the next wave to be magic monsters (much harder, much more loot).
  - Add lineage supports to the Marshall of Zest drop table.
  - Control which womb gifts the player receives.
  - Cause **Zesh** to drop his head; returned to the Genesis tree, it grows a **grass-wing orchid** on the side. Function unspecified — GGG: "you'll have to experiment to find out what this thing is good for".

## Pinnacle access

The pre-existing pinnacle boss **Zest** is now accessed by **communing with the Dreamer** at the monastery, gated behind breach domain completion. (Previously: directly accessible via breach Atlas progression.)

## Lore (0.5)

25 in-world years have passed since the events of the "Keepers of the Flame" content in PoE 1. The war with the Hiveborn has gone badly; they have established a sizeable foothold the keepers themselves cannot penetrate. The player is recruited to do what the order cannot.

## Cross-references with 0.4 baseline

- [[Xesht's Madness]] (Atlas notable, 0.4) — currently grants Onslaught + 50% Effectiveness to rare Breach monsters. Whether this node survives the 0.5 rework is unknown.
- Breach was already promoted to a core Atlas layer in [[patch-0.4.0]]; the 0.5 changes are additive (full quest line + new crafting + new domains) rather than removing the layer.

## Open questions

- Whether 0.4-era Breach Atlas tree notables persist or are replaced wholesale.
- Drop rates / pity timers for hive blood and womb gifts.
- Whether Zest's fight itself is changed or only the access path.
- What the grass-wing orchid actually does — the source explicitly leaves this for player discovery.
