# Path of Exile 2: Breach Rework Recap (0.5 preview)

Official GGG preview video for the [[patch-0.5.0]] "Return of the Ancients" launch (May 29, 2026). Covers the full [[breach]] rework — a wholesale expansion from a single Atlas layer to a multi-stage quest line plus a permanent endgame crafting ecosystem.

Transcript retrieved via youtubetotranscript.com proxy; archived at `raw/videos/Transcript of Path of Exile 2 Breach Rework Recap.md`.

## TL;DR

Breach in 0.5 becomes a multi-act endgame system. Players meet **Alith** of the **Keepers of the Flame**, travel to a **monastery** containing the **Genesis tree** (a stolen Hiveborn artifact), and farm **hive blood** + **womb gifts** to restore the tree's crafting power. Stabilising a breach long enough triggers a new wave phase with tough Hiveborn miscreations that drop hive blood. The full quest line ends with the player penetrating the **Hiveborn foothold** — a new endgame zone of **breach hives** and **skyhives** — and killing the new breach lords **Tool** and **Ash**. Beyond the quest line, breach domains appear randomly on the Atlas, and the existing pinnacle boss **Zest** is now accessed via the monastery.

The [[genesis-tree]] is a new 15-point crafting tree (free respec) that birthens jewelry, including new base types and entire new mod pools — caster mods on rings, minion mods on rings, new amulet bases with built-in spirit skills or rune sockets, jewel catalysts, lightning-damage belts.

## Key claims

### Quest line (one-time)

- First endgame breach: meet **Alith**, one of the [[keepers-of-the-flame]] (ancient order of monks dedicated to preventing the [[hiveborn]] from entering the world).
- Alith recruits the player; sends them south to the monastery.
- Travel to monastery → breaches now show a **visible timer** and visible per-kill duration extensions.
- Monastery contains the **Genesis tree**, gifted by **the Dreamer** — stolen from the Hiveborn.
- Tree is withered; needs **hive blood** to restore.
- To get hive blood: keep a breach portal open long enough to **stabilise** it → new wave phase with miscreations → kill them → drop hive blood. Sometimes attract a **Marshall of Zest** (deadly elite) which drops **womb gifts**.
- 25 years (in-world) since "Keepers of the Flame" event in PoE 1; war with the Hiveborn has gone badly; they hold a sizeable foothold the keepers cannot enter.
- Player uses Genesis tree to craft a **breach stone** from a womb gift, then uses it on the Hiveborn foothold.

### Hiveborn foothold (new endgame zone type)

- Outskirts contain **breach hives** — otherworldly structures the keepers can burn by walking through; each one burnt draws more Hiveborn out. Particularly rewarding for womb gifts.
- Deeper into the domain: **skyhives** — towers securing the Hiveborn's entry into the world.
- Boss kill at the centre of each skyhive: **Tool** and **Ash**, the new breach lords (separate from the existing pinnacle [[zest]]).
- After the quest line, **breach domains** appear randomly on the Atlas; the loop is: breach → womb gift → breach stone → enter domain → kill Tool/Ash.

### Pinnacle access

- **Zest** (existing Breach Pinnacle boss) is now accessed by **communing with the Dreamer** inside the monastery — gated behind doing enough breach domain runs.

### Genesis tree (crafting system)

- 15 allocatable points; **free respec** (toggle nodes on/off).
- Womb gifts slot into the tree's "holes"; each gift creates a specific jewelry type (e.g. amulet womb gift → rare amulet).
- Tree branches control:
  - Base type selection.
  - **New base types** unavailable anywhere else, e.g. amulet with a free spirit skill, or amulet with a rune socket.
  - Mod control: higher-level mods, specific guaranteed mods.
- New mod pools added to rings:
  - **Caster mods** (right branch) — extra elemental spell damage, unleash seal rate, etc.
  - **Minion mods** (left branch) — fills a longstanding gap where minion builds had fewer slots than attack builds for interesting crafting.
- Additional trees for belts and currency (not detailed in the recap). Mentioned: jewel catalyst creation, lightning-damage belt.

### Breach Atlas tree (new nodes)

- Spawn breach hives outside Hiveborn domains.
- Grant **Alith** additional abilities usable while attacking a hive (e.g. force the next wave to be **magic** — much harder, much more loot).
- Add a set of **lineage supports** to the Marshall of Zest's drop table.
- Control which womb gifts the player receives.
- Cause **Zesh** to drop his head; returning the head to the Genesis tree grows a **grass-wing orchid** on the side. The orchid's function is left for the player to experiment with ("putting rings on its fingers seems to make it happy").

### Breach mechanic change

- Visible timer on the open breach.
- Per-kill duration extension is visible.
- Stabilisation phase: keep the breach open long enough → enters a new wave; breach cannot close until the wave is annihilated.
