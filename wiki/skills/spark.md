# Spark

A projectile lightning spell available from level 1. Launches sparks that bounce/travel
and can hit the same enemy multiple times. Central skill of the [[Cold Spark Pathfinder]]
build, where it's converted to cold damage via gear and [[Cold-Infusion]].

## Support gems (Cold Spark Pathfinder)

- Projectile Acceleration III — projectile speed
- Prolonged Duration II — duration (more time for sparks to bounce)
- Fork or Pierce II — extra hits
- Pinpoint Critical — added late, requires Maligaro's Virtuosity to offset crit damage downside

## Scaling priorities

- `+Spell Gem Levels` (biggest scaler — Wand, Amulet, Staff, Focus)
- Spell Damage
- Critical Hit Chance & Critical Damage Bonus
- Cast Speed
- Damage Gained as Lightning / Cold / Fire / Chaos
- Increased Duration
- Increased Projectile Speed

## Buffed by

- [[Cold-Infusion]] — granted by [[Frost Bomb]] and Siphon Elements
- Orb of Storms — deals extra damage whenever you cast inside it

## Notes

- Setup on Weapon Set I (paired with Critical Hit Chance nodes) in the weapon-swap
  setup for the [[Cold Spark Pathfinder]] build
