# Spectral Fire

A spell that produces an explosion. Specifics of cast type, base damage, and tags are not in the currently ingested sources.

## 0.4.0d changes

- Now uses the **player's stats** for calculating its area of effect and dealing damage (previously used some other source — patch notes do not specify).
- Spectral Fire keyword updated to clarify that **damage modifiers do not scale the explosion further** (the explosion is a separate effect from the base damage).

## Implications

The change makes Spectral Fire actually scale with player gear and tree — a build-relevant unlock. The keyword clarification cuts off a double-dipping interpretation: increased damage modifiers do NOT also stack on the explosion. Effective scaling sources are limited to whatever the keyword does count.

## Open questions
