# Tablet Crafting

Tablets modify maps in the player's [[atlas-passive-tree]] region. As of 0.4.0, tablets cap at 4 modifiers and full crafting access is gated behind a pinnacle boss kill.

## Modifier slots

- Slots 1–2: craftable freely.
- Slots 3–4: require [[arbiter-of-ash]] defeated in the current league.

## Modifier pool

In 0.4.0, all modifiers from the generic "Precursor Tablet" can roll on any tablet basetype. The basetype still constrains which content layer the modifiers can affect, but the mod pool itself is unified.

## Modifier categories

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| Item rewards | Collector's (rarity), Bountiful (gold), Elevated (xp) |
| Monster scaling | Challenger's (effectiveness), Teeming (magic), Brimming (rare), Breeding (pack size) |
| Encounter triggers | of Shrines, of Strongboxes, of Essences, of Spirits, of the Exile, of the Summoning |
| Breach | of the Horde, of Rupturing, of Fissuring, of the Invasion, of the Domain |
| Delirium | of Madness, of Persecution, of Mirrors, of Freeze Time, of Phobia |
| Ritual | of Sacrifice, of the Appeal, of the Foundling, of the Cult, of Omens |
| Expedition | of Verisium, of the Detonator, of Knowledge, of Ancient Fiends, of Relics, of Runes |
| Simulacrum | of the Simulacrum |
| Boss-related | of Conquering, of Treasure, of Hoards, of Pathways |
| Map structure | of Undertaking (extra random mods) |

Most existing modifier ranges were widened in 0.4.0 — see the diff table in [[meta/endgame-0.4.0]].

## Modifiers added in 0.4.0

- **Challenger's** — 7–11% increased [[map-effectiveness]].
- **of Contest** — Unique Monsters have 1 additional Rare Modifier.

## Modifiers disabled in 0.4.0

- **Plundering** (item quantity %).
- **of the Pursuit** (delirium reward progress).
- **of Envy** (delirium extra reward chance).

Existing tablets with these modifiers still function but cannot be rolled fresh.

## Strategy notes

- The 4-mod cap forces prioritization. Pack-size + effectiveness + rarity + boss tier is a typical magic-find core.
- The Arbiter gate makes the pinnacle boss kill the actual gateway to optimized mapping, not just a content endpoint.
- 0.5 may change tablet mechanics significantly — see [[patch-0.5.0]] once ingested.
