# Cold Spark Pathfinder Build Guide (Maxroll)

## TL;DR

Maxroll build guide for a Ranger/Pathfinder spellcaster using [[Spark]] as the main skill,
scaled by Cold-Infusions from [[Frost Bomb]] and Siphon Elements. Plays life/evasion through
campaign, swaps to Chaos Inoculation in maps. End-game uses Cast on Critical Comet with
Maligaro's Virtuosity + Pinpoint Critical. Pathfinder ascendancy provides mobility
(immune to slows, cast-while-moving) and mana efficiency.

## Key claims

- [[Spark]] is usable from level 1 and scales primarily with `+Spell Gem Levels`
- Cold-Infusion is the central mechanic — buffs Spark duration, projectile speed, cold damage
- [[Frost Bomb]] + Siphon Elements generate Cold-Infusions; Spell Cascade + Secrets of the Orb = 3 infusions per cast
- [[Pathfinder]] ascendancy node `Path Seeker` lets you respec the Ranger side of the tree to reach Sorceress nodes
- Maligaro's Virtuosity sets Critical Damage Bonus to 250%, enabling Pinpoint Critical without downside
- Cast on Critical must be the LAST gem in the list for weapon-swap to work
- Defensive plan: life/evasion campaign → Chaos Inoculation in maps
- The Whispering Ice (weapon swap) applies Elemental Exposure via Icestorm
- Call of the Brotherhood (Lightning → Cold conversion) increases Cold-Infusion gen from Siphon Elements
