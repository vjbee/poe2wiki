# Lightning Rod

A bow skill that places a Rod at the target location. The Rod deals lightning damage
each time [[Lightning Arrow]] chains to it. Up to 10 Rods can exist at once.

Primary single-target tool of the [[Spiral Volley Deadeye]] build.

## How damage scales

- Each Rod pulses up to 8 times when a Lightning Arrow chains to it
- More Rods on a target → more pulses per arrow → exponentially more damage
- Pre-stacking is essential — the ramp time goes down as you get more sources of
  additional arrows

## Boss damage rotation

1. Pre-stack 20 Rods (placing happens before they reach the 10 cap, decaying ones replaced) BEFORE the boss spawns / becomes vulnerable
2. Optionally place a Tornado Shot (level 41+) for arrow re-emission
3. Barrage once to launch multiple [[Lightning Arrow]] attacks
4. Spam [[Lightning Arrow]] to trigger pulses

For mobile bosses, use Freezing Salvo first to freeze, then combo.

## Ascendancy synergy

- **Endless Munitions** ([[Deadeye]]) — grants an extra projectile to Lightning Rod,
  reducing ramp time substantially

## Weapon swap

Placed on Weapon Set 1 alongside [[Lightning Arrow]] in the build's weapon-swap setup.
