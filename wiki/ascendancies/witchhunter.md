# Witchhunter

Mercenary ascendancy. Companion to [[gemling-legionnaire]]. Identity (per the documented build): a crossbow attacker with strong defensive notables that scale with Evasion and offer Mind-Over-Matter-style damage redirection.

## Known nodes

From the [[Run n Gun Witchhunter]] build guide. The ascendancy is broader than what's documented here.

- **Zealous Inquisition** — leveling power node. Typically **unspecced** after the final Ascendancy Trial; leveling and early-maps tool only.
- **Obsessive Rituals** — mid-stage ascendancy notable.
- **Ceremonial Ablution** — late ascendancy notable. Pairs with the **Sorcery Ward** passive notable to convert 30% of Evasion to HP.

## Recommended order

1. Zealous Inquisition (campaign + early maps).
2. Obsessive Rituals.
3. Ceremonial Ablution.
4. **Unspec Zealous Inquisition** after completing the final Ascendancy Trial — replace with whichever 4th node fits the build.

## Defensive identity

The combo of **Sorcery Ward** (passive tree notable) + **Ceremonial Ablution** (ascendancy) means **30% of the character's Evasion rating is treated as additional HP**. Witchhunter builds therefore lean on Evasion stacking far more than a pure life/armour Mercenary would.

## Builds using Witchhunter

- [[Run n Gun Witchhunter]] — Crossbow attack build; Stormblast Bolts single-target via Mirage Archer auto-proc.
