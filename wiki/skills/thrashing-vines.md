# Thrashing Vines

Druid-class single-target spell available from **level 31**.

## Role

The single-target damage skill in the [[Entangle Oracle]] rotation. Used on rares, mini-bosses, and during boss fights after the [[Entangle]] + [[Thunderstorm]] combo has set up vines on the ground.

## Support linking (per [[Entangle Oracle]] build)

- **Heft**
- **Considered Casting**

## Default Limit

Default Limit is **1**. The Limit caps how many Thrashing Vines can exist simultaneously. Raised via:

- **Idol of Uldurn** (unique amulet — grants additional Limit for single-target skills).
- **Overabundance I** (support gem).

## Scaling

Same offensive priorities as the rest of the build (see [[Entangle Oracle]]):

- +Physical / +Spell Gem Levels (primary).
- %Spell Damage, %Spell Physical Damage.
- Critical Strike Chance + Critical Damage Bonus.
- Cast Speed.
