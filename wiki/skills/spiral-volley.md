# Spiral Volley

A bow attack that fires 29 arrows in a 360° arc, with arrows chaining 6 times between
enemies. One of the most efficient single-click clear skills in PoE 2 — covers ~1.5
screens in every direction.

Central skill of the [[Spiral Volley Deadeye]] build.

## Damage profile

- Requires Frenzy Charges for full damage (a fraction of the damage without them)
- Charge generation is sustained via the [[Resonance]] loop (Armour Break III + Enduring Impact II)
- Bow needs Physical Damage to break armour (only Physical breaks armour)

## Support gems (Spiral Volley Deadeye)

- **Armour Break III** — Endurance Charges on armour break (REQUIRED, build won't work without)
- **Enduring Impact II** — Endurance Charges on Heavy Stun (works on one-shot kills)

Both feed [[Resonance]] which converts to Frenzy.

## Unlock and progression

- Swap to Spiral Volley at level 54 after allocating [[Resonance]]
- Until then, [[Lightning Arrow]] is your main AOE clear
- Place on Weapon Set 2 in weapon-swap setup (order matters for Rigwald's Ferocity)

## Ascendancy interaction

- At level 54, swap Deadeye node Point Blank → **Far Shot** — Spiral Volley benefits more
  from Far Shot since it volleys outward
- 29 arrows means every special unique effect (e.g. Cadiro's Gambit) is guaranteed to roll
  at least one of each effect on a single cast

## Notes

- Hit "at least a few monsters" per cast to reliably regain Frenzies — single-target
  use against one enemy is unreliable for sustain
- Once gear is good enough to one-shot white mobs without Frenzy, the build becomes very
  liberal — no longer need to mind charge sustain on every cast
