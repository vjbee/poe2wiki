# Patch 0.4.0d Patch Notes Preview

## TL;DR

Mid-patch 0.4 league/QoL update (Jan 2026). Heavy focus on the [[fate-of-the-vaal-league]] mechanic — Atziri Temple respawns, Holten placement, room connection fixes, Temple Mod alignment. Outside the league: [[trial-of-chaos]] boss damage tuning, a [[slow-cap]] keyword formalisation (monsters cannot be slowed below 25% base speed), and [[spectral-fire]] now using player stats. No tablet, atlas, ascendancy, or skill-tree balance changes.

## Key claims

- **Fate of the Vaal**: Architect/Atziri fights now allow respawn on death; Holten mechanic placement variance improved and Energized Crystals re-enabled; Atziri's Chambers now connects to any adjacent room; Temple requires at least 1 placed room per run; Temple Mods list aligned with in-map list; Destabilisation keyword clarified (random vs room-completion).
- **Trial of Chaos boss damage**: Bahlak, the Sky Seer overall lowered; Uxmal, the Beastlord overall increased; Vornas, the Fell Flame and Morvak, the Infernal reduced; all Quadrilla monster variants now deal more damage.
- **Slow cap**: Slow keyword updated — monsters cannot be slowed below 25% of base speed. First explicit codification.
- **Spectral Fire**: Now uses the player's stats for AoE and damage. Keyword clarified that damage modifiers do not scale the explosion further.
- **Character sheet**: Now displays item-only armour, evasion, and energy shield values separate from the total.
- **Skills reserving spirit**: Display the final modified reservation value (post-modifiers).
- **Smith of Kitava's Temper Weapon**: Wording clarified — cannot be used with Talismans.
- **Phantasmal Arrow**: No longer targets the ground (bug fix).

No changes were made to atlas passives, tablet modifiers, the Arbiter of Ash, or core endgame loops.
