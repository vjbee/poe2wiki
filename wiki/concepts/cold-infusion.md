# Cold-Infusion

A temporary buff to [[Spark]] (and likely other cold spells) that grants:

- More skill duration
- More projectile speed
- A large amount of cold damage (helps freeze)

## How to generate

- [[Frost Bomb]] grants Cold-Infusions when it explodes
- Siphon Elements (spirit gem) has a chance to grant the matching infusion when you
  inflict an elemental ailment — freeze gives Cold-Infusion
- With Spell Cascade, Frost Bomb generates 2 Cold-Infusions per cast
- Allocating `Secrets of the Orb` on the passive tree adds +1 (3 total per cast)

## Synergy items

- Call of the Brotherhood — converts Lightning Damage to Cold, increasing chance of
  Siphon Elements granting a Cold-Infusion specifically

## Related

- Fire-Infusion works the same way but for fire spells; Living Bomb generates them
  to buff Comet damage (~2x) in the [[Cold Spark Pathfinder]] end-game setup
