# Thunderstorm

Druid-class spell available from **level 14**. Acts as the detonation trigger in the Entangle + Thunderstorm + Accelerated Growth combo: hits onto active [[Entangle]] vines cause large explosions.

## Role

- **Combo trigger**: cast over [[Entangle]] vines after they have spread.
- **Recommended support**: **Shock Conduction II**.
- In the [[Entangle Oracle]] endgame setup, Thunderstorm is kept at a **low gem level** for cost efficiency — its job is to trigger the combo, not to scale damage on its own.

## Build use

Currently documented in [[Entangle Oracle]].
