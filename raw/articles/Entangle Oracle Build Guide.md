## Core playstyle

Cast **Entangle** to spread vines everywhere before "watering" them with **Thunderstorm**. Combined with the **Accelerated Growth** support gem, the vines create massive explosions. **Thrashing Vines** handles single-target.

## Primary skills

### Entangle (core ability, level 1)
- Auto-targeting vine spreading.
- Linked to: Accelerated Growth support gem.

### Thunderstorm (detonation trigger, level 14)
- Triggers Accelerated Growth explosions on Entangle vines.
- Support recommendation: Shock Conduction II.

### Thrashing Vines (single-target, level 31)
- Linked to: Heft, Considered Casting.
- Default limit: 1. Increasable via Idol of Uldurn or Overabundance I support.

### Early-campaign alternatives (levels 1–14)
- Orb of Storms + Volcano + Spark.
- Arctic Armour (Uncut Spirit Gem, level 4).
- Mana Remnants (Uncut Spirit Gem, level 4).

## Oracle ascendancy nodes (in order)

1. **The Unseen Path** — reveals special nodes.
2. **Entwined Realities** — allocate high-value notables without pathing.
3. **The Lesser Harm** — reduce hit damage; lowers crit chance.
4. **Forced Outcome** — reroll crits at "25% – 35%" investment for damage scaling.

## Keystones

- **Wildsurge Incantation** — early campaign priority.
- **Mind Over Matter** — endgame conversion mechanic.
- **Eldritch Battery** — converts Energy Shield to Mana.

## Key notables

- Boundless Growth, Constricting, Cut to the Bone (Armour Break source), Scarred Faith, Blackflame Covenant, Blood Transfusion (Life-casting transition), Sanguimantic Rituals (Arcane Surge conversion), Aspiring Genius (Arcane Surge), Calculated Hunter, Give Up Your Essence.
- Undying Hate jewel (Tecrod variant) → Sacrifice of Blood.

## Unique items

- **Bushwhack** (early) — physical damage converts to Pin effect; Pin duration 3 seconds.
- **Idol of Uldurn** (early/mid; amulet) — grants additional Limit for single-target skills.
- **Atalui's Bloodletting** (endgame, lineage support) — Lifetap + Life-scaling damage.
- **Atziri's Rule** (endgame, staff) — cultivates Life leech on spells; corrupted with "5% of Spell Damage Leeched as Life".
- **Kurgal's Gaze** (endgame, helmet) — doubles Life regeneration node values.
- **Fast Metabolism** (implicit, instilled on amulet, endgame).

## Defensive layers

- Early campaign: armour stacking, "armour applies to elemental damage", Life-based.
- Endgame conversion: Life-based casting via Atalui's Bloodletting and Lifetap → enables Mind Over Matter + Eldritch Battery (Energy Shield → Mana acting as additional health, since damage hits Mana before Life).
- Resistance cap 75% all elements.

## Sustain mechanics (endgame)

"In endgame the high Life cost of our spells combined with our Mana taking damage from Mind Over Matter + Eldritch Battery makes sustain very important."

- Mana Remnants (supported by Clarity I, Vitality I).
- Thunderstorm kept at low level for cost efficiency.
- Arcane Surge (from Aspiring Genius) → converted via Sanguimantic Rituals.
- Sacrifice of Blood scaling (Undying Hate variant).

## Offensive scaling priorities

1. +Physical Gem Levels, +Spell Gem Levels (primary damage source).
2. %Spell Damage, %Spell Physical Damage.
3. Critical Strike Chance & Critical Damage Bonus.
4. Cast Speed.
5. Damage Gained as Lightning/Cold/Fire/Chaos.
6. Increased Duration (plant duration).
7. Increased Area of Effect (overlap/clear).

## Skill rotation

### Single target
1. Temporal Chains (pre-boss).
2. Cast Entangle multiple times.
3. Thunderstorm over Entangle.
4. Thrashing Vines spam (boss active).

### Clear
1. Entangle (multiple casts).
2. Thunderstorm over Entangle.
3. Thrashing Vines on rares/bosses.

## Leveling progression

- **Levels 1–14**: Orb of Storms + Volcano + Spark. Acquire Thunderstorm.
- **Levels 14–31**: Entangle + Thunderstorm combos; grab Shock Conduction II. Acquire Thrashing Vines.
- **Levels 31+**: Full three-skill rotation. Path toward Wildsurge Incantation keystone.

Passive priority (campaign): Spell damage + cast speed (early); Plant nodes (Boundless Growth, Constricting); Cut to the Bone (Armour Break); Wildsurge Incantation keystone.

## Endgame stages

### Late campaign / early maps
- Begin Life-cost conversion.
- Staff with +Physical/Spell Gem levels.
- Focus stats: resistances, attributes, Life, Armour, cast speed.
- Budget: a couple of divines for Atalui's Bloodletting (deferred).

### Endgame (Mind Over Matter + Eldritch Battery)
- Atalui's Bloodletting (converts Entangle cost to Life; adds damage).
- Atziri's Rule with "5% of Spell Damage Leeched as Life" corruption.
- Fast Metabolism instilled on amulet.
- Kurgal's Gaze (helmet).
- Undying Hate (Tecrod variant) near Blackflame Covenant.
- Goal: mitigate Mind Over Matter / Eldritch Battery downsides via Life/Mana sustain.
