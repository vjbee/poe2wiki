# Frost Bomb

A delayed-explosion cold spell. Deals significant damage on its own early on, but its main
role in the [[Cold Spark Pathfinder]] build is generating [[Cold-Infusion]] for [[Spark]]
when it explodes.

## Support gems (Cold Spark Pathfinder)

- Short Fuse I — faster explosion
- Overabundance I — more bombs
- Spell Cascade — 2 Cold-Infusions per cast

With `Secrets of the Orb` allocated on the passive tree, total = 3 Cold-Infusions per cast.

## Notes

- Applies Elemental Exposure (lowers enemy resistance), which combines with The
  Whispering Ice's Icestorm for big single-target damage windows
- In the weapon-swap setup, sits on Weapon Set II — paired with Exposure Effect and
  Secrets of the Orb nodes on the passive tree
- Rotation: pre-stack Frost Bomb to build infusions before engaging
