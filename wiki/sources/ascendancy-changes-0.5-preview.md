# You NEED To See These Ascendancy Changes (0.5 preview)

Official GGG preview video for the [[patch-0.5.0]] "Return of the Ancients" launch (May 29, 2026). Covers two ascendancies in detail: **Gemling Legionnaire** (Mercenary) and **Chronomancer** (Sorceress).

Transcript retrieved via youtubetotranscript.com proxy; archived at `raw/videos/Transcript of You NEED To See These Ascendancy Changes.md`.

## TL;DR

Gemling gets a new ascendancy skill (**Advanced Thaumaturgy**) that adds a second quality bonus to every socketable skill gem, with extreme examples like Fireball going from 2 to 10 projectiles. Also gets a new defensive skill, **Virtuous Barrier**, which scales with the attribute requirements of socketed gems. Chronomancer gets a new notable (**Phased Form**) that delays incoming hit damage, and the delayed portion can be erased by **Temporal Rift**. Several existing Chronomancer nodes were reworked: Inevitable Agony, Rapid River, Time Snap, Time Freeze, Now and Again.

## Key claims

### Gemling Legionnaire (Mercenary ascendancy)

- **Advanced Thaumaturgy** (ascendancy skill) — every socketable skill gem in the game now has an additional quality option, unlocked when this node is allocated. Hold Alt while hovering a gem to preview the bonus.
- Example: **Fireball** — instead of firing 2 additional projectiles, fires "up to 10 in a circle". With Fire Infusion, the ring of projectiles explodes into another ring of smaller fireballs.
- Example: **Explosive Shot** — gains 2 more rounds in its magazine.
- **Virtuous Barrier** (new ascendancy skill) — a barrier conjured from socketed skill gems. Each socketed gem empowers it, scaling with the gems' required attributes.
- "Over 200 different skill gems" are affected by the new quality bonuses.

### Chronomancer (Sorceress ascendancy)

- **Phased Form** (new notable) — take less damage from hits; a portion of that damage is taken after a delay. The mechanic only applies to hits, so damage over time is "just straight up mitigated".
- **Temporal Rift** interaction — using Temporal Rift after a big hit not only recovers life to its prior value but **also erases the delayed damage** stored by Phased Form.
- **Inevitable Agony** (existing skill, reworked) — new visuals and gameplay; displays stored damage to bosses on their static life bars. When stored damage reaches a lethal level, the effect triggers immediately to kill the boss.
- **Rapid River** (passive) — removed. Recoup-speed investment has been redistributed across the core passive tree, opening Recoup to more builds.
- **Time Snap** and **Time Freeze** — both can now be allocated without spending all 8 ascendancy points (previously required full investment).
- **Now and Again** (reworked) — your spell casts now have a chance to alter time itself and both **echo** and **repeat**. Both rolls are independent — you can get both at once.
