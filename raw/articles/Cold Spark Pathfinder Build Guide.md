## Cold Spark Pathfinder Build Guide for Path of Exile 2 The Last of the Druids 0.4.0

The Cold Spark Pathfinder for the Ranger class shows just how versatile this ascendancy can be. This build is a spellcaster instead of using a bow, the Ranger's traditional weapon.

Shattering everything on the screen with Cold-Infused Sparks and raining down Fire-Infused Comets. The Cold Spark Pathfinder has amazing clear, mobility, and single target. But the best part has to be the mobility. Casting while moving, being immune to all slows, and not having the annoying stun from being hit while sprinting! These are all the perks of being a Pathfinder caster.

Early on you play a life/evasion hybrid build, leveraging the safety granted by Evasion to easily make your way through maps. Later on you switch over to Chaos Inoculation to access a larger effective health pool and become immune to Chaos Damage.

This build doesn't require any Unique items to function early on. But the build does benefit from several Uniques such as Call of the Brotherhood to generate more Cold-Infusions from Siphon Elements and Maligaro's Virtuosity to set your Critical Damage bonus to 250% and negate the downside of Pinpoint Critical.

## Skills

Early Access - Last of the Druids

Cold Spark Pathfinder Build Guide

- Straight from level 1, you can use the build's main ability, Spark.
- Spark should be supported by utility supports such as Projectile Acceleration III, Prolonged Duration II, and Fork or Pierce II. Much later Pinpoint Critical comes into play.
- Early on Spark does not do that well by itself, it needs to be supported by a few other spells.
- Frost Bomb is the behind the scenes MVP of this build. It does a LOT of damage early but it's mainly used to generate Cold-Infusions, which it does after exploding.
- Cold-Infusions buff Spark in almost every way, more duration, projectile speed - and they grant Spark a ton of cold damage, which great for freezing enemies.
- With this in mind, link it with Short Fuse I and Overabundance I as soon as you can. Later add in Spell Cascade, this gives you 2 Cold-Infusions per cast. You can gain an additional Cold-Infusion (for 3 total), by allocating Secrets of the Orb on the Passive Tree.
- Orb of Storms is up next. This ability works great with Spark because it deals damage whenever you stand in it and cast other abilities. Link this to Overabundance I and Unleash early on.
- For your Spirit Gem, grab Arctic Armour early on, this helps to stop enemies from surrounding you, and occasionally actuallys kills them!

- Siphon Elements is a Spirit Gem that gives another way to generate Cold-Infusions for Spark. This ability is not to be underestimated, in combination with Frost Bomb you should always have some Cold-Infusions up.
- Siphon Elements has a chance to give an infusion when you shock, freeze, or ignite an enemy. It gives the infusion which corresponds to the ailment you inflicted, so when you freeze an enemy you may get a Cold-Infusion.
- With this in mind, Call of the Brotherhood is a great item to use before you have a lot of damage gained as cold gear.
- This item converts Lightning Damage into Cold Damage, giving you a much higher chance of getting a Cold-Infusion from Siphon Elements.
- Mana Remnants is another important Spirit Gem for this build.
- Whenever you kill an enemy with elemental ailments, or critically hit an enemy with elemental ailments, you have a chance to get a "Mana Remnant" these remnants give a ton of mana, and even overflow your mana above the normal maximum.

- Once you reach the End Game, you need to meet a few gear and stat requirements before you can start using Cast on Critical Comet.
- The first and most important requirement is in order to even use Cast on Critical is to have enough Spirit. This build uses a Sceptre to achieve this - you can use a Rare Sceptre or something like Guiding Palm of the Eye but the important thing is that it grants you enough Spirit to use Cast on Critical.
- ==MAKE SURE Cast on Critical IS LAST ON YOUR GEM LIST THIS IS IMPORTANT FOR WEAPON-SWAP TO WORK LATER!==
- The next requirement, and as important as the first, is to have enough Critical Hit Chance for Cast on Critical to work properly. This is where Pinpoint Critical comes into play on Spark and you should also put on Maligaro's Virtuosity on at the same time. Cast on Critical gains energy whenever you deal a Critical Hit and the amount of energy gained is dependent a few things such as damage of the hit and things like Freeze Buildup.
- Once you meet these requirements you can start using Cast on Critical Comet! And in this case also Living Bomb.
- Living Bomb should be kept at the minimum level for mana costs to be lower, it's sole purpose in this build is to generate Fire-Infusions, which buffs the Comet to deal almost double damage!

- Cast on Critical Comet requires a TON of mana in order to properly work. This build deals with it in two ways: mana efficiency nodes from Pathfinder ascendancy and Quipolatl's Thesis glove augment which grants mana regeneration based on the cost of "unactivated" curses.
- Elemental Weakness is used early on, the most important support gems for this to work are Spell Cascade, and Ritualistic Curse.
- Spell Cascade triples the number of curse that are out at one time, and Ritualistic Curse makes the activation delay even longer!
- This should also be used with Whispers of Doom on the passive tree.
- You can take this even further by using a Spellslinger Wand to cast one curse and to self-cast spam another curse such as Despair, this is really only worth doing once you have very high Comet level however, and also the Min-Max set-up which deals a significant amount of chaos damage.

## Ascendancy

Early Access - Last of the Druids

Cold Spark Pathfinder Build Guide

- The order of the Ascendancy nodes depends on if you have leveling gear or not. IF you have leveling gear you should take Relentless Pursuit from your first trial and Running Assault from your second trial for mobility. IF you do NOT have leveling gear, Path Seeker from your first trial and Traveller's Wisdom from your second trial gives more damage. The following order assumes you have leveling gear.
- Relentless Pursuit makes you immune to ALL slows, removing annoying map modifiers such as Chilled Ground and Temporal Chains.
- Running Assault grants a TON of mobilty through the 50% reduced movement speed penalty while casting, which is amazing for spells such as Spark. The best part is it also removes that annoying heavy stun if you happen to get hit while sprinting!
- Path Seeker is a game-changer, this ascendancy node allows you to remove the entire Ranger side of the tree to grab the much more efficient Sorceress side of the tree, while still maintaining the option to take movement speed nodes from Ranger start.
- Traveller's Wisdom works best when you have attributes on gear, this ascendancy allows you to change the +5 attribute nodes into 5% global defense, damage, or mana efficiency. This build mostly takes the mana efficiency, but global defense is great as well, and damage can be very good early on, especially with items such as Astramentis.

- For the attribute nodes take enough ==Intelligence==,==Dexterity====,====and== ==Strength== to meet gear, skill gem and support gem requirements.
	- You can use your Gold at the Hooded one or Doryani to respec and change your Attributes if a new skill or gear upgrade leaves you unable to use your skills/items.
- **Early Campaign** focus on getting as much increased projectile damage, spell damage, and elemental damage as possible. Important nodes to go for early for damage are Thin Ice and Heavy Frost.
- Heavy Frost is VERY impactful, with this node, you ignore all resistances. This is particularly helpful for generating more Cold-Infusions while clearing. Freeze Buildup in general is a very important stat to look out for on the passive tree.
- Projectile Speed, Increased Skill Effect Duration, and Cast Speed are also very important for scaling Spark, nodes like Kite Runner, Effervescent, Turn the Clock Forward, and Preservation are very important!
- When entering **Maps** Critical Hit Chance and Critical Hit Chance for Spells are the next stats to focus on.
- Setting up your Weapon Swapping tree adds a ton of damage through Exposure effect on Frost Bomb and eventually Icestorm from The Whispering Ice.
- Set Spark on Weapon Set I, and Frost Bomb on Weapon Set II. Weapon Set I points are focused on Critical Hit Chance nodes, and Weapon Set II nodes are focused on Exposure Effect and things like Secrets of the Orb, which is VERY important for Frost Bomb! When set up properly through the ingame UI, these skills are used automatically with the correct weapons and passive tree.

- During the campaign, get as much Resistance and Life as you can.
- There are a few uniques that you can use for Spark that lasts until maps and greatly speed up the campaign.
- Painter's Servant gloves grant a bunch of damage gain and converts your spark into all three elements - which is great for freezing.
- Crown of the Victor adds +1 skill gems which is not to be underestimated. Life and Mana on kill are also great!
- Enfolding Dawn combined with Threaded Light gives a ton of early Spell Damage, which is important since Ranger start only has projectile damage.
- Grip of Kulemak can be great for damage with things like Cast Speed and Damage Gain - but be warned, the reduced light radius makes the minimap smaller!
- Astramentis is more on the expensive side... but this item pairs great with Traveller's Wisdom by granting massive +attributes.
- Make sure to check vendors for +Lightning Spell Skill Gems and +Spell Skill Gems on Staff, Wand, Amulet, and Focus, this is most of your damage scaling early on!
- You should instill Secrets of the Orb early on if you can afford it, otherwise Acceleration is a cheap alternative.[Equipment](https://maxroll.gg/poe2/planner/wplvo0ur)

Disciple of Varashta Plant Campaign Gear

- As you enter the Atlas of Worlds and begin mapping, your first goal is to cap your resistances and meet Attribute requirements. You want as much Energy Shield as you can get as soon as possible.
- A majority of your Energy Shield comes from gear, especially body armor, make sure to keep an eye out for this especially!
- Prioritize a Wand with +Spell Gem Levels as well as a Sceptre for spirit. Ideally Guiding Palm of the Eye for damage gained as cold. Corrupted versions of this are much cheaper. A rare sceptre with mana, +str/int, and extra spirit can be used as well.
- Cast Speed on Rings and Weapon helps out immensely as well.
- Call of the Brotherhood is great early on for generating extra Cold-Infusions, though this can be dropped at higher gear levels.
- Maligaro's Virtuosity socketed with Quipolatl's Thesis is very important for this build!
- Maligaro's Virtuosity sets your Critical Hit Damage Bonus to 250%, this is very important in order to use Pinpoint Critical, which allows you to reach enough Critical Hit Chance for Cast on Critical to start working properly.
- After that, focus on increasing your life, energy shield, and armor.[Equipment](https://maxroll.gg/poe2/planner/wplvo0ur)

Disciple of Varashta Plant Early Map Gear

- The Cold Spark Pathfinder scales extremely well with gear, and can get very expensive. This part of the build is by no means required, but should give you some ideas for where to go after the Maps gear.
- Headhunter is a chase unique, so a cheap alternative would be Ingenuity. Remember that the better your rings are, the more value you get from Ingenuity.
- Palm of the Dreamer is makes Spark's already amazing clear even better by adding Chaos Explosions, this can be combined with Tul's Stillness Lineage Support, which causes chaos damage to also freeze.
- The Vertex corrupted with +2-4 Skill Gems can be a MASSIVE damage increase, this makes mana more of a problem however, and also makes you less tanky because the helmet slot grants a lot of Energy Shield.
- Atziri's Splendour can be used with Chaos Inoculation only IF you remove the mana costs converted to life cost modifier - this item is very expensive with good rolls. You are looking for high Evasion/Energy Shield, ideally as much Energy Shield as possible.
- Rite of Passage and The Fall of the Axe are also chase items.[Equipment](https://maxroll.gg/poe2/planner/wplvo0ur)

## Summary

Early Access - Last of the Druids

Cold Spark Pathfinder Build Guide

- The Cold Spark Pathfinder combines the absurd clear speed of Spark with the absurd mobility of Pathfinder.
- Shattering the entire screen, and raining down Comets on bosses, all while sprinting freely and without worry of getting stunned.
- Scale levels as much as possible with your Wand, Amulet, and other gear. This is where most of your damage comes from!
- Scale Energy Shield and pick up Chaos Inoculation, this combined with Evasion and Global Defences from Traveller's Wisdom makes you quite tanky!
- The Cold Spark Pathfinder build has a very satisfying playstyle with skills that have great quality of life and is perfect for doing all content currently in Path of Exile 2!
