# Resonance

A keystone passive that converts charges between types. Most relevant effect: **Endurance
Charges are converted to Frenzy Charges** (and Power Charges generated by other sources
also feed into Frenzy via Called Shots in the [[Deadeye]] setup).

This keystone is the central engine of the [[Spiral Volley Deadeye]] build, enabling
self-sustaining Frenzy generation that would otherwise require dedicated skill slots.

## How it enables Spiral Volley Deadeye

1. [[Spiral Volley]] is linked to Armour Break III (Endurance on armour break) and
   Enduring Impact II (Endurance on Heavy Stun)
2. Resonance converts those Endurance Charges into Frenzy Charges
3. The next Spiral Volley cast is empowered by the new Frenzies it just generated
4. Loop sustains itself as long as the cast hits enough targets

## Requirements

- Build does NOT work without this keystone allocated
- Build also does NOT work without Armour Break III + Enduring Impact II linked to Spiral Volley
- Bow needs at least decent Physical Damage — only Physical Damage breaks armour

## Fallback charge generation

- Snipe linked to Perfected Endurance generates the first Frenzy when you run out
  (keep Snipe at level 3 for lower mana cost)
- Called Shots auto-casts Sniper's Mark which grants Power Charges → Charge Profusion II
  occasionally grants Endurance Charges → Resonance turns them into Frenzy

## Synergy items

- Serpent's Egg — triples charge generation; "as soon as you can afford" priority
- Anointing Serpent's Egg with Leaping Ambush carries crit chance in the crit-swap setup
