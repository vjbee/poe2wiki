# Breach domain (Hiveborn foothold)

A new endgame zone type introduced in [[patch-0.5.0]] as part of the [[breach]] rework. Entered by applying a **breach stone** (crafted at the [[genesis-tree]]) to a Hiveborn foothold on the Atlas.

**Status:** 0.5.0 preview.

## Zone structure

A breach domain is layered into two main areas:

### Outskirts — breach hives

- Otherworldly structures the Keepers of the Flame can burn by walking through them.
- Each one burnt draws more Hiveborn to attack the player as they purge the hive.
- "These encounters are particularly rewarding for womb gifts" — primary farming target for [[genesis-tree]] crafting fuel.

### Inner — skyhives

- Twisted towers deeper into the domain that secure the Hiveborn's entry into the world.
- Each skyhive contains a boss fight at its centre.
- Bosses: **Tool** and **Ash** — the new breach lords (separate from [[zest]], the existing pinnacle).

## Endgame loop

After completing the one-time quest line that introduces breach domains:

1. Find a breach stone via [[genesis-tree]] crafting.
2. Enter a breach domain on the Atlas (spawns randomly).
3. Destroy skyhives.
4. Kill Tool and Ash to clear the foothold.
5. Repeat for any new breach domain that appears.

This loop is the main breach endgame in 0.5 — separate from the existing pinnacle access to [[zest]], which is now mediated through **the Dreamer** at the monastery.
